
# Deer Park Plumbing - Website-as-a-Service (WaaS) Plan

## A) Project Summary
- **Business**: Deer Park Plumbing and Maintenance Inc.
- **Target Customer**: Homeowners and business owners facing urgent plumbing issues (leaks, clogs, heater failures).
- **Service Area**: Pasadena, Deer Park, La Porte, Baytown, and Channelview, TX.
- **Conversion Goal**: Drive immediate phone calls through high-visibility CTAs and trust-based local positioning.

## B) Site Structure & Section Order
1.  **Header**: Sticky navigation with click-to-call.
2.  **Hero**: Bold headline, immediate offer (Free Inspection), and primary Call CTA.
3.  **Trust & Social Proof**: Star ratings, "Licensed & Insured" badges.
4.  **Services**: Grid of top solutions with "Emergency" highlighting.
5.  **Why Choose Us**: Value propositions (Local, Experienced, Transparent).
6.  **Service Area Coverage**: List of cities with localized keywords.
7.  **Reviews**: Customer testimonials emphasizing speed and professionalism.
8.  **FAQ**: Addressing common friction points.
9.  **Contact / Call Section**: Final conversion push.
10. **Footer**: Business info, license details, and site links.
